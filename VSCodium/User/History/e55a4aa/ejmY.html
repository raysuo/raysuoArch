BUILDING BRIDGES

BETWEEN CULTURES, PEOPLE AND GENERATIONS

ECHOES is a global network of young people committed to creating a more peaceful, sustainable and inclusive future. We unite voices and ideas to design international initiatives that promote cooperation across cultures and generations. We are a hub born from a desire for social innovation where young people drive change with events, debates and concrete proposals. Together, we amplify the echo of our voices to inspire a better tomorrow, built by young people for the whole world.

Our mission is to empower a global network of young changemakers who are committed to peace, sustainability, and meaningful connection. We aim to create a future where young people are actively engaged, connected, and equipped to drive positive change in their communities and around the world.

Our association is dedicated to connecting young people through a variety of activities, which include workshops, events and conferences that promote informal education and advocacy and Hackathons that foster international cooperation and innovation. By encouraging open communication and raising awareness on important issues, we empower youths from diverse backgrounds to share their insights and develop essential skills for their personal and professional journeys.
